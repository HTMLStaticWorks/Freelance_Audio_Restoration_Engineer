Project: 1950s Jazz Archive

The challenge with this project was a 1954 original pressing that had significant surface noise, deep scratches causing loud pops, and a consistent 60Hz hum from the original recording equipment.

Technical Approach

  • High-resolution 96kHz/24-bit digitization using a premium moving coil cartridge.
  • Automated de-clicking followed by manual surgical repair of deep scratches.
  • Spectral de-humming to remove the 60Hz interference without affecting the bass frequencies.
  • Subtle EQ enhancement to restore the original "air" lost to time.
