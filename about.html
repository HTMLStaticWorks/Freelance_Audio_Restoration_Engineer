About PureWave

We are a specialized audio engineering studio dedicated to the art and science of restoration.

Our Mission

Founded by senior engineer Alex Reed, PureWave began with a simple goal: to rescue the world's damaged audio heritage. From forensic evidence to lost musical masterpieces, we treat every signal with the utmost respect and technical precision.

Our studio combines vintage analog listening sensibilities with state-of-the-art spectral processing tools. We don't just "filter" noise; we surgically reconstruct audio artifacts to reveal the hidden clarity within.

Our Philosophy

The PureWave Standard

We adhere to the highest standards of audio engineering, prioritizing fidelity, authenticity, and precision above all else.

Sonic Authenticity

We believe in preserving the original artist's intent. Our restoration removes the noise without coloring or degrading the underlying performance.

Data Integrity

From forensic evidence to unreleased master tapes, we maintain strict chain-of-custody protocols and enterprise-grade encryption for all client files.

Technological Edge

We constantly invest in the latest AI-assisted spectral processing algorithms, combining them with world-class analog playback hardware.

Our Laboratory

Acoustically Perfected Environment

You can't fix what you can't hear accurately. Our global facilities are purpose-built with floating floors, non-parallel walls, and custom acoustic diffusion to provide a perfectly flat listening response.

  • ATC & PMC reference monitoring systems
  • Studer & Ampex calibrated tape machines
  • Prism Sound A/D and D/A conversion
  • Dedicated iZotope RX Advanced workstations
