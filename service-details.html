Spectral Repair & Artifact Removal

Identify and eliminate discrete audio artifacts visually through spectral analysis. From mouth clicks to distant sirens, we fix it all.

What is Spectral Repair?

Unlike traditional EQ or broad noise reduction, spectral repair allows us to "see" sound in a two-dimensional frequency map. This enables our engineers to surgically remove unwanted sounds that overlap with the audio you want to keep.

Dialogue Restoration

Remove lip smacks, heavy breathing, and plosives without affecting the speaker's tone.

Intermittent Noise

Eliminate cell phone interference, coughs, or chair squeaks during critical takes.

Our Methodology

We utilize a multi-stage workflow to ensure the highest possible quality:

  • Initial high-res spectral analysis to map out artifacts.
  • Manual attenuation of discrete noises to maintain natural timbre.
  • Phase-coherent reconstruction of missing frequencies.
  • Final mastering check for consistent tonal balance.
